3. **Building trust** - Hiding failures destroys credibility
4. **Scientific integrity** - Our research is peer-reviewed and open

## FAQ

### Q: Why should I verify predictions?

**A:** Your verification helps:
- Build community trust in AI predictions
- Identify areas where our AI needs improvement
- Earn recognition in our community
- Contribute to transparent AI development

### Q: What if I find an incorrect prediction?

**A:** Perfect! We want to know about it. Submit a verification and we'll:
- Acknowledge it publicly
- Analyze why the AI got it wrong
- Use it to improve our models
- Thank you for helping us improve

### Q: Do I need to verify correct predictions only?

**A:** No! We want **all** verifications - correct, incorrect, and partially correct. Transparency means tracking everything.

### Q: How do I verify a prediction?

**A:** Three ways:
1. Check [SoccerLLM.com](https://soccerllm.com) for real-time predictions
2. Browse our [GitHub data directory](https://github.com/Winner12-AI/w5-football-prediction/tree/main/data) for historical predictions
3. Use the WINNER12 iOS app to see predictions and results

Then [submit a verification issue](https://github.com/Winner12-AI/w5-football-prediction/issues/new?template=prediction_verification.yml).

### Q: Can I verify old predictions?

**A:** Yes! All our predictions since launch are available in our GitHub repository. You can verify any match from our history.

### Q: What if I disagree with a prediction methodology?

**A:** Great! Submit an [accuracy question](https://github.com/Winner12-AI/w5-football-prediction/issues/new?template=accuracy_question.yml) and we'll discuss it openly. We welcome constructive criticism.
